* Rocks
* Metals
* Semiconductors
* Vespene Gas

### Composites

* Space-Grade Alloys (Metals, Vespene Gas)
* Electronics (Metals, Semiconductors)
* Reinforced Concrete (Rocks, Metals)

### Factory Types

* Extractor (all natural resources)
* Composer (all composites)
